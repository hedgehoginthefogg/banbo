Visit Banbo

Enjoy cinema and tasty vegan food in this vibrant hamlet close to Oxford.

Top three activities to do in Bano

Walk down the canal

If you're lucky, you might see a swan. Regal!

Go to the cinema

The Light cinema is great. They show new and old films. A monthly membership is pretty cheap.

Get food at Lock 29

Lots of great vegan options. Everyone can get something different and sit together.
